WeRateDogs 🐶 Twitter Exploratory Data Analysis 📊 2015 - 2017 📅

By Mohamed Elsayed Zaky Elsayed 2024
WeRateDogs is a popular Twitter account that humorously rates and comments on user-submitted photos of dogs. With a substantial following, the account's unique blend of humor and positivity has garnered widespread attention. This exploratory data analysis (EDA) focuses on tweets from WeRateDogs between 2015 and 2017, providing insights into trends, engagement metrics, and patterns within the data. The analysis aims to uncover how various factors, such as tweet content, time of posting, and dog characteristics, influence the popularity and engagement of tweets during this period. By examining this data, we can better understand the elements that contribute to the success and appeal of the WeRateDogs Twitter account.
